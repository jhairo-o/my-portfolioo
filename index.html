Career Path Research

Chosen Career: ARCHITECHTURE

I chose to pursue architecture as my career path because it allows me to combine my creativity, design, and problem-solving skills. Since architects design buildings and spaces that are both functional and aesthetic, this career interested me because it gives me the opportunity to help develop environments and create spaces that can improve the quality of people's daily lives.

Possible Courses

As a future architect, I can take a Bachelor of Science in Architecture (BS Architecture) in college. This program teaches architectural designing, building technology and drafting. After achieving the degree, graduates usually take the Architect Licensure Examination to become a licensed architect.

Contribution to the Economy

Architects contribute to national development by designing safe and sustainable buildings such as homes, schools, hospitals, and offices. Their work helps improve infrastructure, support urban development and create spaces that benefit communities.

As a Filipino citizen, future architects like me can help communities affected by the climate conditions in the country. We can help make strong buildings that won't easily be taken down my the natural disaster's effects, which can really help develop the Philippine's economy by improving the lives of its people.
